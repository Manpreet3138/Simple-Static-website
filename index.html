Some common questions we’re often asked

What are the environmental impacts of plastic bags?

Plastic bags contribute to pollution, harm marine life, and take hundreds of years to decompose.

How many plastic bags are used worldwide each year?

Over 1 trillion plastic bags are used annually.

How can individuals reduce plastic bag usage?

Use reusable bags, refuse single-use bags, and recycle plastic bags.

How long does it take for plastic bags to decompose?

Typically 100–500 years or more.
